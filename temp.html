Max Temperature

There is a strong, non-linear correlation between a city's latitude and temperature as illustrated by the scatter plot above. As you can see, the max temperature increases steadily as latitude approaches 0 (at the equator) where it plateaus. The max temperature then begins to decline steadily as each city moves further from 0 latitude. You will also notice that as you move further from the equator the range of temperatures for any given latitude steadily increases. This plot illustrates what we know about climate relative to the equator and latitudinal position.
